How do I add an iCloud album?

  1. Open Settings (tap the screen, then tap the gear icon)
  2. Go to "Manage Sources"
  3. Tap "Add iCloud Album"
  4. Load available albums and select your desired album
  5. Enter a name and tap "Add"

How do I connect to a Samba/NAS server?

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to "Manage Sources"
  3. Tap "Add Samba Share"
  4. Enter server address (e.g., 192.168.1.100), share name, username, and password

Images are not displayed

Check the following:

  • Is at least one source enabled?
  • Did you grant the app access to your photos? (iOS Settings ‚Üí Picture Frame Mate ‚Üí Photos)
  • For Samba: Are the credentials correct?
  • For iCloud: Does the album contain photos?

How does Night Mode work?

Night Mode dims the screen or turns it completely off:

  • Sunset to Sunrise: Uses your location for automatic times
  • Custom Time: Choose start and end times manually
  • Dim: Reduces brightness
  • Black Screen: Turns the display completely off

Can I use multiple sources simultaneously?

Yes! You can add as many iCloud albums and Samba shares as you like. The app intelligently mixes images from all active sources.

The iPad turns off automatically

The app automatically prevents auto-lock while running. If the problem persists, check iOS Settings under "Display & Brightness" ‚Üí "Auto-Lock".
